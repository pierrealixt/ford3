OpenEdu

An open and free education resource database for South Africa

What is OpenEdu?

OpenEdu is an initiative to provide a complete and well maintained database of higher education providers and the courses and qualifications that they offer.

OpenEdu is non-commercial and unaffiliated to any particular service provider.

OpenEdu provides an open API (a platform that allows service providers to easily fetch and share the data we curate).

Social Impact

Being able to discover further education opportunities is a critical need for learners planning their career paths. In turn, higher education opportunity data is a key resource to ensure that those who wish to provide career guidance facilities so learners have the information they need at their fingertips.

Our Misson

Our goal is to enable an ecosystem of service providers who provide useful and current education career path guidance and advice to learners. We provide this service and data for free and without restriction in order to facilitate social upliftment through higher education.

Who will benefit from OpenEdu?

Learners

Find accurate and relevant information about further education opportunities so that you can plan your career path.

Educational institutions

Recruit more students by advertising your qualifications on the OpenEdu platform.

Service providers, Governments, NGO

Use the OpenEdu data and API to build meaningful applications for South African students.
